📄 Paper Template

All papers must follow the IEEE two-column conference paper format. Use the official IEEE template available for LaTeX and Microsoft Word.

  • Maximum 6 pages including figures, tables, and references
  • Font: Times New Roman, 10pt
  • Margins: Standard IEEE conference margins
  • All figures must be high resolution (300+ DPI)
  • Paper size: US Letter (8.5" × 11")
IEEE LaTeX Template Word Template

📤 Submission Steps

  1. Prepare your paper using the IEEE template
  2. Convert to PDF format — maximum file size 10 MB
  3. Ensure all fonts are embedded in the PDF
  4. Remove author information for double-blind review
  5. Submit via the online submission system -> Submit Paper
  6. Await reviewer notification by 30 September 2026

🔍 Plagiarism Policy

IICCCS-2026 follows strict IEEE plagiarism policies.

  • Similarity index must be below 20% overall
  • No more than 2% from a single source
  • Papers with self-plagiarism above threshold will be rejected
  • Use iThenticate or Turnitin for pre-check
  • All submissions will be verified using IEEE CrossCheck

📸 Camera-Ready Instructions

After acceptance, submit your camera-ready paper by 20 October 2026.

  • Address all reviewer comments thoroughly
  • Complete IEEE copyright transfer form
  • Ensure paper passes IEEE PDF eXpress validation
  • Author names should be fully disclosed in final version
  • Add IEEE Conference ID 61609 in the footer

🔬 Review Process

All submitted papers undergo rigorous double-blind peer review by at least 3 independent reviewers from the Technical Program Committee.

  • Reviews based on originality, significance, clarity, and relevance
  • Decisions: Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, Reject
  • All decisions are final after revision round
